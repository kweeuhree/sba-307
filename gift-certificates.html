Sam Ash Gift Certificates
Give the gift of music by purchasing an e-Gift Certificate online from SamAsh.com or a Gift Card at any Sam Ash Music retail location. Both are redeemable online at samash.com, or over the phone at 1-800-4-SAMASH, or at any Sam Ash Music retail location. Best of all, Sam Ash Gift Cards and e-Gift Certificates never expire and have no maintenance fees, so your friends or loved ones can use theirs whenever they want!

Sam Ash Gift Cards
Just visit any Sam Ash Music Store to purchase a gift card. Sam Ash Gift Cards are available in denominations of $5 to $1500.

Sam Ash e-Gift Certificates
Just use the form on the left in the e-Gift Certificate section, choose any whole dollar denomination from $10 and up, and your chosen musician will receive a personalized SamAsh.com e-Gift Certificate via email within 24 hours* of your purchase.

If you need to purchase an e-Gift Certificate now, but wish to deliver it later, follow these steps:

  • Place your e-Gift Certificate order as you would normally, but instead of sending it directly to the recipient, have it sent to yourself.
  • After you receive the email confirmation of the e-Gift Certificate number and PIN/Security Code, save this information.
  • When you're ready to give the gift, just forward the e-Gift Certificate emails to the recipient, or print the saved information and present it in person. It's that easy!
